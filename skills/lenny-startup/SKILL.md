# Lenny Skills: Startup

5 actionable skills for startup founders.

## Skills

### 1. Designing Team Rituals
- Create rituals that reinforce values
- Keep them lightweight
- Evolve as the team grows

### 2. Fundraising Strategy
- Tell a compelling story
- Know your metrics cold
- Build relationships before you need money

### 3. Startup Pivoting
- Recognize when to pivot
- Preserve what's working
- Move quickly and decisively

### 4. Sales Compensation Design
- Align incentives with goals
- Keep it simple
- Adjust as you learn

### 5. Sales Qualification
- Define your ideal customer
- Qualify early and often
- Don't waste time on bad fits
