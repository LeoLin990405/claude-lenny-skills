# Lenny Skills: Sales & Partnerships

4 actionable skills for sales and business development.

## Skills

### 1. Founder Sales
- Founders should do early sales
- Learn what resonates with customers
- Build the playbook before hiring

### 2. Building Sales Team
- Hire for coachability
- Define clear sales stages
- Create repeatable processes

### 3. Enterprise Sales
- Understand the buying committee
- Navigate procurement
- Build executive relationships

### 4. Partnership & BD
- Align on mutual value
- Start with small pilots
- Document everything
