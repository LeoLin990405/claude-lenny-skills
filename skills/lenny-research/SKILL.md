# Lenny Skills: User Research

4 actionable skills for understanding your users.

## Skills

### 1. Conducting User Interviews
- Ask open-ended questions
- Focus on past behavior, not future intent
- Listen more than you talk

### 2. Designing Surveys
- Keep it short (5-10 questions max)
- Use a mix of quantitative and qualitative
- Avoid leading questions

### 3. Analyzing User Feedback
- Categorize by theme
- Quantify frequency and impact
- Separate signal from noise

### 4. Usability Testing
- Test early and often
- Use think-aloud protocol
- Focus on tasks, not opinions
