# Lenny Playbooks - Role-Based Skill Combinations

Curated collections of Lenny skills for specific roles and career stages. Each playbook combines essential skills from the base Lenny skill categories.

## Available Playbooks

### 1. Startup Founder Playbook
**For:** First-time founders, early-stage startup leaders
**Stage:** Pre-seed to Series A

**Core Skills (from lenny-* categories):**
- `lenny-startup`: Fundraising Strategy, Startup Pivoting, Designing Team Rituals
- `lenny-hiring`: Writing Job Descriptions, Conducting Interviews, Building Team Culture
- `lenny-strategy`: Defining Product Vision, Writing North Star Metrics, Prioritizing Roadmap
- `lenny-growth`: Measuring Product-Market Fit, Designing Growth Loops
- `lenny-sales`: Founder Sales
- `lenny-execution`: Shipping Products, Managing Timelines

**Key Focus Areas:**
1. Find product-market fit before scaling
2. Build the founding team
3. Raise capital strategically
4. Ship fast and iterate

## How to Use Playbooks

### Quick Start
Ask me to help with a specific playbook:
- "Use the Startup Founder playbook to help me with fundraising"
- "Apply the PM playbook to write a PRD for [feature]"
- "Use the First-Time Manager playbook for my first 1:1"

### Combining Skills
Playbooks automatically combine relevant skills. For example:
- "I'm a founder preparing for Series A" → Combines startup, hiring, strategy, and communication skills
- "I'm a new PM at a growth-stage company" → Combines PM, growth, and execution skills

### Custom Playbooks
You can request custom combinations:
- "Create a playbook for a technical PM at an AI startup"
- "What skills do I need as a PM transitioning to founder?"
