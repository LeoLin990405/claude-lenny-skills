# Lenny Skills: Marketing & Brand

5 actionable skills for marketing your product.

## Skills

### 1. Positioning & Messaging
- Define your category
- Articulate your unique value
- Test messaging with customers

### 2. Brand Storytelling
- Lead with the customer's problem
- Show transformation
- Be authentic and consistent

### 3. Launch Marketing
- Build anticipation before launch
- Coordinate across channels
- Have a post-launch plan

### 4. Content Marketing
- Create content that solves problems
- Optimize for search and sharing
- Build a content flywheel

### 5. Community Building
- Start with your most engaged users
- Create value for members
- Foster peer-to-peer connections
