# Lenny Skills: Design & Engineering

5 actionable skills for design and engineering excellence.

## Skills

### 1. Platform & Infrastructure
- Build for scale before you need it
- Invest in developer experience
- Monitor and measure everything

### 2. Engineering Culture
- Foster psychological safety
- Encourage experimentation
- Celebrate technical excellence

### 3. Design Systems
- Start with components, not pages
- Document usage guidelines
- Evolve based on real usage

### 4. Running Design Reviews
- Focus on user outcomes
- Give specific, actionable feedback
- Separate critique from approval

### 5. Design Engineering
- Bridge design and engineering
- Build interactive prototypes
- Ship pixel-perfect implementations
